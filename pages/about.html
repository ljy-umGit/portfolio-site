Hi, this is Junyan

I'm a Product Designer with 4 years of experience in designing for both 2C and SaaS products. I'm driven by my empathetic personality to create designs that balances user experience and business impact.

Graduated from California College of the Arts with a Master's in Interaction Design, and a background in Industrial Design; I continue to equip myself with domain knowledge & skills to facilitate cross-disciplinary collaboration, and align design visions with business requirements.

My goal is to make the users' day whenever someone cross path with my work; whether it's at a job or chilling at home. Currently, I'm working to bridge gaps between traditional tech education and industry demands; that said, I'm always open to new adventures.
